My story as trainer of dragon

When I was a kid. I picked up a egg in the road. Then, I bring it back to home. Subsequently, the eggs split up several pieces. A little dragon with eggshell go outside.Then, I start to learn how to train a dragon on the internet.

After years of hard work, the dragon learn to fly and fire. The dragon grow up to a very large size, which even cannot contained into house. The dragon takes me fly to remote places.

The End.
